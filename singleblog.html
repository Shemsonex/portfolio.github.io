I have visited Mystic Hot Springs several times since my first visit with my kiddos over five years ago. Since then, I have visited with just me and my kiddos two times, on a girl's trip getaway, with my partner on a romantic getaway, and as a family one other time.

All our visits have been in the winter, and every visit has been filled with fun moments that I will remember and cherish forever.
